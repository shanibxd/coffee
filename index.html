OUR STORY

Fresh Roasts & Kettle Brews

At Coffee Brown, we bring you the comfort of home with a touch of craft. Our coffee beans are sourced from the volcanic shade-grown estates of Chikmagalur and roasted locally to bring out rich, chocolatey notes.

Every cup of tea is boiled fresh to order in brass kettles, infusing hand-crushed ginger, green cardamom pods, and organic Assam leaves. To complete your afternoon, our sweets counter **Brown Laban** serves unique regional confectioneries crafted by traditional halwais.
